y = np.random.rand(N)

Goal: Simulate a simple dice game where two players roll a die 10 times. Track wins and declare a winner.

Steps:

  1. Use np.random.randint() to simulate 10 rolls for each player.
  2. Compare outcomes roll by roll.
  3. Score 1 point per win per round. Ties = 0 points.
  4. Print final scores and declare the winner.
